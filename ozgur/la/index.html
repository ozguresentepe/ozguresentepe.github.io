Informationen

Exams: There will be two exams in this lecture. The date and time will be announced after they are finalised. The exams will be out of 30 points. Students will need 30 points out of a total 60 in order to obtain a positive grade.

Make up exam: There will be a make up exam in February. This exam will cover the content of the entire course. It will be out of 30 points and it will replace the weaker exam from the semester.

Presentations: Before each lecture, a problem sheet will be posted and students will be expected to have worked out solutions to these problems when they arrive at the lecture. Students will be asked to present solutions on the board. This will be on a voluntary basis. For each (correctly) presented solution, 2 points will be awarded. Students will need at least 2 points from the presentations to obtain a positive grade.

Lectures: Attendance is mandatory as per university rules.

Grades: A total of 32 points (30 from the exams and 2 from the presentations) is needed to obtain a positive grade.
57-64: Grade 1 -- 49-56: Grade 2 -- 41-48: Grade 3 -- 32-40: Grade 4
