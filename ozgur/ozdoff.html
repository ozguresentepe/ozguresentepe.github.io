About

In the Summer of 2022, I saw a tweet which said "If you're an academic without a website, you don't exist." It upset me. So, I made this small thing to list personal academic webpages of my friends in order to make sure that they are alive. You can submit your answers by filling this google form. -- If we are not really friends, you can buy me a coffee and just write "I once bought you a coffee" as an answer to how we know each other in the google form. -- If you do not have a personal webpage, I made this thing which you may use to create a simple one. -- I shuffle the list every couple days. -- I was inspired by commalg.
