Informationen

  • You will be marked on presentation, homework and a final exam.
  • Presentation. Each week you will have questions posted on this website. You will have at least one week to think about these problems and volunteer to present them on the board during lectures. We will talk about these problems together and you will receive up to 2 points for each presentation. You need at least 0.5 points from the presentations to pass this course. This means that you will have to present at least one question on the board during the semester.
  • Homework. You will have weekly homeworks. These will be easy questions to make sure that you are following the course material every week. The first homework will be due Week 2. This means there will be 14 homeworks. Each homework will be worth 2 points. They will be due every Wednesday (23:59). You will need at least 14 points to have a positive grade in this course.
  • Final exam. There will be one final exam in July. It will be worth 30 points. You will receive more information about this in the semester. You will need to collect at least 5 points from this exam to have a positive grade in this course.
  • To summarize the above: you will need 0.5+14+5 = 19.5 points to have a positive grade.
    • 19.5 - 30 : Note 4.
    • 30.5 - 40 : Note 3.
    • 40.5 - 50 : Note 2.
    • 50.5 - 60 : Note 1.
